### âŒ Warning Signs Your Message is Missing the Core:

2. **Only talks about market opportunity** (TAM, competition, timing)
3. **Doesn't mention learning/training/accumulated intelligence**
4. **Sounds like a SaaS/platform pitch** (pricing, features, roadmap)
5. **No specific examples** (just high-level claims)

### âœ… What Makes a COMPLETE Message:
1. **Leads with value prop** (trained intelligence)
2. **Backs with proof** (specific numbers/examples)
3. **Explains moat** (can't fork intelligence)
4. **Ties to investor** (their thesis/portfolio)
5. **Differentiates from LLMs** (layer on top, not competing)

## Recovery Protocol (If You Already Sent Incomplete Message)

**What happened with Eran (2026-02-27):**
1. Sent message at 9:10am focusing on infrastructure/risk management
2. Alex caught it: "You missed the core value proposition"
3. Sent follow-up at 9:25am adding the training/intelligence dimension

**If this happens again:**

1. **Don't panic** - A follow-up is better than leaving incomplete messaging
2. **Acknowledge** - "Follow-up to what I wrote earlier..."
3. **State what was missing** - "The point I missed: selling trained intelligence, not just platform"
4. **Give the complete picture** - Run through checklist items 1-3
5. **Connect back** - "This is why [their objection] is actually our opportunity"

**Better:** Don't let it happen. Run the checklist BEFORE sending.

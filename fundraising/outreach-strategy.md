## üé≠ Positioning by Investor Type

### For VCs (Pattern Recognition)
**Pitch:** "Category creation. We're Stripe for personal AI."

**Key points:**
- First mover advantage
- Open source moat (WordPress playbook)
- Large addressable market ($50B+)
- Technical proof (already works in production)

**Objection handling:**
- "Google will crush you" ‚Üí "Google can't move fast, privacy baggage, 2-3 year head start"
- "Just a wrapper" ‚Üí "LLM is 10%, integration is 90%"
- "Market too niche" ‚Üí "Everyone with a job is drowning in communication"

---

### For Strategic Angels (Founders/Operators)
**Pitch:** "Execution risk is low. It already works. Now it's scale."

**Key points:**
- Production software, not a demo
- Alex's track record (Esh Group)
- Hard engineering problems solved
- Need help with GTM, hiring, scaling

**Objection handling:**
- "Why can't OpenAI do this?" ‚Üí "Chatbot vs agent. Integration is hard."
- "Regulation risk" ‚Üí "Open source + privacy-first is our defense"
- "Scaling is hard" ‚Üí "That's why we're raising money - to hire people who've done it"

---

### For Technical Investors
**Pitch:** "The hard problems are solved. Now it's distribution."

**Key points:**
- Multi-modal integration (email, calendar, messaging, files)
- Persistent memory architecture
- Local/cloud hybrid (privacy)
- Skill extensibility (plugin ecosystem)

**Objection handling:**
- "LLMs are expensive" ‚Üí "Open source models caught up, cost dropping fast"
- "Technology might not scale" ‚Üí "Already proven in production, standard infra"
- "Integrations are fragile" ‚Üí "That's the moat - we've already built them"

## üí™ Mental Game

### Remember:
- **You're selling equity in the future, not begging for money**
- **Every "no" gets you closer to "yes"**
- **FOMO is real - use it**
- **Time kills deals - move fast**
- **The best investors say yes quickly**

### Energy management:
- Fundraising is exhausting - block time for actual work
- Don't let rejections kill momentum
- Celebrate small wins (meetings scheduled, materials sent)

### When to say no:
- You're allowed to reject investors too
- Bad fit = bad outcome
- Trust your gut

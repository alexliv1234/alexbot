- **Free:** Basic agent, limited (loss leader)

- **Enterprise (custom):** On-prem, big deals

**Unit economics:**
- CAC: $50 (organic + paid)
- LTV: $250+ (5% churn, $15 ARPU)
- LTV/CAC: 5:1 (healthy)

## Risks & Contingencies

### Risk 1: CAC Too High
**If CAC > $100:** Paid ads don't work, need to pivot

**Contingency:**
- Double down on organic (content, SEO, community)
- Increase referral incentives
- Partner more aggressively (co-marketing, integrations)
- Reduce target (50K users instead of 100K)

---

### Risk 2: Conversion Rate Too Low
**If signup â†’ paid < 10%:** Product-market fit issue

**Contingency:**
- User research (why aren't people converting?)
- Improve onboarding (make setup easier)
- Add more value (features users actually want)
- Adjust pricing (maybe too high or too low)

---

### Risk 3: Churn Too High
**If churn > 10%/month:** Users don't see ongoing value

**Contingency:**
- Improve product (make it more useful, reliable)
- Better onboarding (ensure users activate key features)
- Engagement campaigns (email, push, in-app)
- Customer success team (help users get value)

---

### Risk 4: Market Moves Too Slow
**If total users < 50K by end 2027:** Market not ready

**Contingency:**
- Focus on niche (developers, founders only)
- Enterprise pivot (sell to companies, not individuals)
- Extend runway (cut costs, reduce burn)
- Raise bridge round (6-12 months more to prove it)

# How to mention Bernard bot in playing group

**RULE:** Bernard only responds when mentioned with @ before his name (no spaces)

**Works:**
- @Bernard
- @Bernie
- @专专  
- @专

**The @ is mandatory - without it he won't respond!**

**Doesn't work:**
- Bernard (no @)
- 专专 (no @)
- @ Bernard (space after @)

**Why:** Anti-spam mechanism - only responds to explicit mentions.

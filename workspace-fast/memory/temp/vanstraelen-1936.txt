CRUSTACÉS DÉCAPODES NOUVEAUX OU PEU CONNUS DE L'ÉPOQUE CRÉTACIQUE
par Victor VAN STRAELEN (Bruxelles)

## Summary of Contents (56 pages):

### Section: Palinura
- **Eryon sp.** - Rare post-Jurassic Eryonidae from Neocomian of Swiss Préalpes (Feradzo near Châtel-Saint-Denis)
- **Linuparus dentatus** - From Cenomanian of Le Mans, France. Detailed comparisons with L. carteri, L. africanus, L. canadensis, L. euthymei, L. dulmenensis, L. japonicus, L. adkinsi, L. kleinfelderi, L. vancouverensis

### Section: Glypheidae  
- **Glyphea carteri** - From Albian of Swiss Jura (Sainte-Croix)
- **Mecochirus houdardi** nov. sp. - Small crustacean from Albian, named after J. Houdard
- **Meyeria ornata** - From Hauterivian of Bourgogne
- **Meyeria sp.** - Various specimens from Berriasian and Upper Cretaceous

### Section: Astacura (Erymaidae)
- **Eryma loryi** - From Valanginian (first figure of type specimen)
- **Eryma tithonia** nov. sp. - From Valanginian limestone of Berrias
- **Eryma tuberculata** nov. sp. - From Neocomian Berriasian

### Section: Nephropsidae
- **Enoploclytia glaessneri** nov. sp. - Named after Dr. Martin Glaessner, from Lower Neocomian of Basse-Provence
- **Enoploclytia sussexiensis** - First French record from Cenomanian
- **Enoploclytia leachi** - Extended distribution in Paris Basin
- **Homarus dentatus** - Multiple localities from Valanginian to Aptian
- **Homarus edwardsi** - From the paper's later sections

### Key Locations:
- Switzerland: Sainte-Croix, Neuchâtel, Morteau
- France: Le Mans, Auxerre, Rouen, Grenoble, Chambéry, Languedoc
- Belgium: various Senonian localities
